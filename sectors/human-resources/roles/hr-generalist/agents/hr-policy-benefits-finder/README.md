# HR Policy & Benefits Finder

A Microsoft 365 Copilot declarative agent that helps employees and HR teams find policy information, benefits details, and HR procedures.

## Overview

| | |
|---|---|
| **Sector** | Human Resources |
| **Role** | HR Generalist |
| **Complexity** | Low |
| **Setup Time** | ~20 minutes |
| **Capabilities** | OneDriveAndSharePoint |

## What it does

- **Answer policy questions** — PTO, leave, remote work, expense policies
- **Explain benefits** — health, dental, 401k, enrollment
- **Clarify procedures** — how to submit requests, who to contact
- **Find handbook info** — any topic in the employee handbook
- **Direct to resources** — forms, contacts, next steps

## What it doesn't do

- Process requests or approve exceptions
- Access individual employee records
- Make policy decisions
- Provide legal advice

## Friction addressed

| Friction Type | Time Saved |
|---------------|------------|
| Repetitive policy inquiries | 2.5 hrs/week |
| Information seeking | 1 hr/week |
| Benefits questions | 0.5 hrs/week |

## Deployment options

**Option A: HR Team Only**
- HR generalists use to answer employee questions faster

**Option B: All Employees (Recommended)**
- Self-service for common questions
- Reduces HR inquiry volume significantly
- 24/7 availability for policy information

## Quick start

1. **Gather** current employee handbook, benefits guides, policies
2. **Configure** manifest with SharePoint URLs
3. **Deploy** to HR team or all employees
4. **Test** with common policy questions

See [SETUP.md](SETUP.md) for details.

## Example conversations

> "How much PTO do I get per year?"

> "When is open enrollment and how do I sign up?"

> "What's the policy for parental leave?"

> "How do I submit an expense report?"

## Requirements

- Microsoft 365 Copilot license
- SharePoint access to HR documentation
- Current employee handbook and policy documents
